PALAGI

For me this song is about unconditional love and commitment in a relationship. The song expresses the idea that no matter what happens—arguments, challenges, or misunderstandings—the love remains strong and unchanged.emphasizing the promise of staying together through ups and downs. It’s a reassuring song about choosing to love someone every day, despite the difficulties that come with relationships.
